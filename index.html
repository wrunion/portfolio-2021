NYC Social Service Search

In 2020 I completed the JavaScript/React Front End Development Program at Epicodus in Portland, Oregon.

For my Capstone Project, I found an open data API with data about 1000+ agencies that provide social services for women in NYC. Then I created the NYC Women's Social Service Resource Finder.

Social services are a passion of mine, but information about these opportunities can be tricky to find, and confusing to access. Which is where my passion for UX in social service websites comes in!

The NYC Social Service app allows users to search by up to three different categories, plus a New York neighborhood, then see resources that meet those requirements.

View the demo | View the code

It's a bite-sized demo of what I do in my work now with the Mapping Action Collective - I collaborate with non-profits to get resources to as many people as possible, using data and design.

Rose City Resource Guide

The Rose City Resource Guide is a collaboration between the Mapping Action Collective (MAC), and the Portland non-profit Street Roots. It's a searchable map of free and low-cost resources for houseless adults in Oregon.

I work at the Mapping Action Collective, and help maintain the site. I'm also creating technical documentation for Street Roots staff, and redesigning the UI for a brighter and more modern feel.
